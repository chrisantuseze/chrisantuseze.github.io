Chrisantus Eze

I'm a Computer Science PhD student at the Robotic Cognition Lab in Oklahoma State University advised by Christopher Crick. My research focuses on robot learning for manipulation, computer vision, Imitation learning, and reinforcement learning.


Chrisantus Eze is a third year PhD student at Oklahoma State University working on robot learning. He received his B.S. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from FUTO, Nigeria, and immediately after graduation joined Seamfix Limited as a Software Engineer where he worked for three years. His research agenda are in two folds; to develop agents who are capable of learning and carrying out robust manipulation skills, and to be able to adapt to changing environments and to manipulate a variety of objects.
